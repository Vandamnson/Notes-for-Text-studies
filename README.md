reading of pure-math textbooks.

## Contents

More chapters / books to be added as they go.

## A note on the PDFs

Files in this repo are downsampled exports of iOS handwriting-app pages
(originals are continuous-scroll, often hundreds of MB). Pages are
sliced to roughly Letter aspect for normal scrolling/printing. Originals
available on request.
